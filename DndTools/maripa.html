Maripa

In the fallout of the necromancers war, the last god divested their divine spark power to mortals in Maripa. These mortals became the ruling families, Maria, Velez, and Costa. They use their Sorcerous powers to protect the country alongside the Royal Guard, powerful paladins that maintain order. The city was origianally built on a delta, but as the delta eroded away over the centruies the city has moved to docks. Artificial sandbars with small fortresses surround the city.
