Vallahirstead

In the North of the Forsaken Lands, even more North than the Shio, lies vallahirstead, the shattered city. Called both the shattered city and the violet city for the floating amethyst shards that float of above city, this city hosts not one, but three of the greatest centers of magical learning. Vallahirstead was once only an outpost of Krandel, but after that nation was overrun by demons in 506 ANW, it became a city state, powerful in its own right. At the center of the city lies a lake that is held by the bottom half of an enormous amethyst geode, the source of the magical floating rock. Despite the cold, the lake never freezes over. This is not all that strange though, for wizards fly through the sky on flying carpets, and even the lowliest of storekeepers knows a bit of magic.

Mage's Guild

The first school built in the sky. Though it has a reputation for producing more dark lords than archmages, this school is a great place for those seeking powerful and forbidden knowledge.

The most prestigious school in all of Vallahirstead, this school is only available to either the very talented or very rich, and those able to graduate here are guaranteed to be both skilled and well connected. This flying school was built in the shape of a Roc.

Advanced Arcanist Alliance

This school welcomes those that are seeking an education that is both quality and affordable. It has classes above and below.

A great place to learn how to craft magical items. This school floats in the air and is shaped like a big donut.

This school accepts all and graduates few, but for any wanting to learn the secrets of arcane engines, this is a good place to start

Umbral Flame

The oldest school in Vallahirstead.

Named for a time when the city was new, this school boast some of the best religious and historical courses.

With a long tradition of divination magic, this is one of the best schools in the world for those seeking to know the unknowable and find that which is lost.
